About HOG Christian Network Network

Head of Grace Christian Network is a church based in Newcastle Upon Tyne, dedicated to advancing the Christian faith and proclaiming the Gospel of Jesus Christ for the benefit of the public in the United Kingdom and abroad. We do this in accordance with our Statement of Faith, through sermons, praise, worship, prayer, religious education, Christian fellowship, outreach and pastoral care. We seize every opportunity to lift Jesus high so all men can draw near (John 12:32).
