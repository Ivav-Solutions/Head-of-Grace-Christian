About Us

We’re Heirs, Anchored by His Love, Spearheaded by His Grace

Head of Grace Christian Network is a church based in Newcastle Upon Tyne, dedicated to advancing the Christian faith and proclaiming the Gospel of Jesus Christ for the benefit of the public in the United Kingdom and abroad. We do this in accordance with our Statement of Faith, through sermons, praise, worship, prayer, religious education, Christian fellowship, outreach and pastoral care. We seize every opportunity to lift Jesus high so all men can draw near (John 12:32).

Our Mission

Head of Grace Christian Network sets to echo the presence of Jesus through the preaching of His words, draw men into salvation of an eternal life in Christ Jesus, and enjoy our inheritance by His grace.

Message from our Lead Pastor ‘Adedayo Falusi’

From the very beginning, God’s unmerited grace chose us, creating us in His own image and granting us dominion over the earth—a privilege given to no other part of creation. Though flawed, we remained the focus of His love and grace. When sin threatened our relationship with Him, His love moved Him to sacrifice His only Son, Jesus Christ, offering us total redemption for all who believe and receive Him.

This boundless love and grace do more than just redeem us—they sustain us, making us heirs of an eternal Kingdom. As His chosen, we’re called to carry this light forward.

Statement of Faith

We believe in the Bible as the inspired and only infallible Word of God.

We believe in the gifts and fruits of the Holy Spirit.

We believe in the personal return of Jesus Christ.

We believe in one God, eternally existent in three persons: Father, Son, and Holy Spirit.

We believe in the baptism of the Holy Spirit with the evidence of speaking in tongues.

We believe in water baptism as a public confession of accepting Jesus, a symbolism of the joint death and resurrection with Christ Jesus.

We believe in the deity of our Lord Jesus Christ, His virgin birth, His atoning death, and His bodily Divine resurrection.

We believe that Christ is for all who accepts Him and in His teachings to spread love to all mankind regardless.
